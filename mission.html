MISSIONS

  1. Try to shoot all the targets when you play the levels.

  2. Try not to shoot your friends while you shoot your enemies and always try until you cross a level.

  3. Try to cross a level only in 1 shoot.

  4. Try to shoot within 10 seconds to learn agility.

  5. Try to cross all levels within 10 shoots.

  6. Try to use advanced armory weapons.
